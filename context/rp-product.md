# RemotePass — Product Context

## What it is
B2B HR and payroll platform for remote teams. Companies hire and pay global workers through RemotePass.

## Two user types
- **Client** — the company (HR admin, finance team). Manages contracts, payroll runs, documents.
- **Worker** — the remote employee. Receives payments, manages personal documents, tracks time.

## Client navigation (10 sections)
Activity · People · Payroll · Invoices · Bill Pay · Reports · Time Tracking · Documents · Transactions · SpendCards

## Key product concepts
- **EOR (Employer of Record)** — RemotePass acts as the legal employer on behalf of the client
- **Contractor** — worker hired directly, not through EOR
- **Payroll run** — monthly process of calculating and approving worker payments
- **Bill Pay** — client pays invoices through the platform
- **SpendCards** — company expense cards for workers
